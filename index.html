Key Inputs

  • Up Arrow ↑ - when pressed will increase the speed the snow falls.
  • Down Arrow ↓ - when pressed will decrease the speed the now falls.
  • Left Arrow ← - when pressed will decrease the amount of snow on the screen
  • Right Arrow → - when pressed will increase the amount of snow on the screen
  • R Key R - when pressed will reload the page if pressed (Use if snow or smoke doesn't appear/move)
